Frequently asked questions by new engineering graduate students at UCLA

What are the best ways to find housing while at UCLA?

There are several options available to graduate students at UCLA. A short list of resources include:

  • University apartments

    https://housing.ucla.edu/student-housing/graduate-students-and-students-with-families/living-in-university-apartments

  • UCLA Off-campus housing system

    http://www.data.cho.ucla.edu/

  • Padmapper

    http://www.padmapper.com/search/apartments/California/los-angeles/

  • UCLA Co-op housing

    http://www.uchaonline.com/

Where is the eGSA lounge, and how do I get in?

The eGSA lounge 2438 BH, and requires a key-code to enter. This code varies by department, and was distributed at the beginning of your time at UCLA, but you may reacquire it by emailing lounge@bruinegsa.org.

I am applying for jobs, are there resources available on campus to help?

  • Graduate student resource center (GSRC)

    http://gsrc.ucla.edu/

  • Graduate student writing center (GSWC)

    http://gsrc.ucla.edu/gwc/
