Staying updated with eGSA events

  • Weekly emails: you should already receive them when are enrolled via Courseweb. If not, please contact secretary@bruinegsa.org.
  • eGSA Facebook page

    • Regularly scheduled events

  • Townhall (quarterly): learn about events for the upcoming quarter and provide feedback/suggestions while enjoying free food.
  • Engineering Social (quarterly): enjoy free food and beer and meet other engineering MS/PhD students!
  • Volunteering (monthly): join in humanitarian work for an evening and meet other like-minded engineering graduate students interested in community service.
